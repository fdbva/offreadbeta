Welcome to OffRead!


Here you can go offroad and keep reading on the browser, here how it's done:


1 - Copy a story link from a supported site

2 - Paste it in the input on our side bar menu

3 - Click Scrape if you only want to save it on your device


Done! You can start reading as soon as it loads.


Optionally, you can also click on the Scrape+{googleDriveIcon} to save it in the cloud.


For now, these are our supported sites:

www.fictionpress.com

www.fanfiction.net
